Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 20, 2024

Auto Refresh & Clicker does not require a user account and does not collect personal information such as your name or email address.

The Safari extension stores its settings locally in your browser. It does not send browsing history, page contents, keywords, clicks, or page text to our server.

We do not use analytics SDKs inside the Safari extension. If you contact support by email, your email address and message are used only to respond to your request.
